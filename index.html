A Better Nutmeg World
Love is rarely a straight line it moves like the ebb and flow of the world. Skies collapse in thunder and rain, then open wide, Warm sunshine stretching across the plains. There are no promises of steady skies, no guarantee of only gentle days but knowing I have you beside me is more than enough to fight, more than enough to stay. If love is a battlefield, then our victory cannot be won alone. It must be cultivated, together as one deriving a profound happiness, outliving both space and time. When you have your doubts Come back to this. No matter if the skies are blue, or when the storms come in, know these two scriptures, always constant and true: the world is a better place because of you. And more importantly, my world is better because it is with you.
— Written and Deployed For Nutmeg from Steviekins
